The Laboratory

关于实验室

Alchymia Labs is the quantitative research arm of The Alephain Guild, dedicated to the systematic transmutation of market chaos into structured alpha.

Alchymia Labs是阿莱夫行会的量化研究部门，致力于将市场混沌系统性地转化为结构化的Alpha。

Our name draws from the ancient art of alchemy — the precursor to modern chemistry. Where alchemists sought to transform base elements into gold through precise processes, we seek to transform raw market data into refined trading insights through rigorous quantitative methodology.

我们的名称源自古老的炼金术——现代化学的先驱。正如炼金术士通过精确的工艺将贱金属转化为黄金， 我们通过严谨的量化方法将原始市场数据转化为精炼的交易洞察。
